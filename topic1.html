Understanding Climate Change

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. Human activities, especially the burning of fossil fuels, significantly contribute to global warming.

Causes

  • Greenhouse gas emissions
  • Deforestation
  • Industrial pollution

Effects

Rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and biodiversity loss.
